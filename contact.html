Frequently Asked Questions

Need Answers?

How do I register my team for a tournament?
You can register your team by navigating to the Tournaments page, selecting an active tournament, and filling out the registration form. Ensure your captain submits the correct contact email.
What payment methods are accepted in the shop?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and select cryptocurrencies for global accessibility.
How are the global rankings calculated?
Rankings are calculated based on a proprietary Elo system that factors in tournament placements, match win rates, and opponent strength over a rolling 12-month period.
Can I appeal a ban on the community forum?
Yes, you can submit an appeal ticket via the support email listed on this contact page. Please include your username and the reason for the ban.
